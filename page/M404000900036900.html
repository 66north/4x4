UNDERBODY ANTICORROSION AGENT LOCATIONS


In order to provide a greater corrosion resistance, underbody corrosion prevention treatment should be performed after undercoat application. After completing body repairs, restore this underbody corrosion prevention treatment as indicated in the illustration if necessary. Recommend wax:

  • Tectyl 506T, 506 or S
  • Waxoyl
note
  • Take care not to permit the anticorrosion agent to come in contact with body painting, brake parts, exhaust pipes, bearing seals for rear axle and rambler parts (such as tires and drive shaft boots).
  • If anticorrosion agent gets onto the oxygen sensor, it will cause a drop in the performance of the sensor. Cover the oxygen sensor with a protective material when applying anti-corrosion agents and undercoats.
