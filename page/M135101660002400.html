Code No.54: Motor relay system (open circuit, short circuit or motor relay coil defect)
















OPERATION



  • The motor relay 1 and the motor relay 2 are controlled by the ASTC-ECU.
  • If the brake fluid pressure in the hydraulic brake booster (HBB) becomes low, the ASTC-ECU outputs the motor relay actuating signal to the motor relay 1 and the motor relay 2 and then the pump motor operates.
  • The ASTC-ECU monitors the pump motor (at 25 terminal).

DIAGNOSIS CODE SET CONDITIONS


Diagnosis code No.54 is set if the motor relay circuit is open or short, the motor relay 1 or the motor relay 2 is defective, or the internal circuit of the ASTC-ECU is defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the motor relay 1 or the motor relay 2
  • Damaged wiring harness or connector
  • Malfunction of the ASTC-ECU
