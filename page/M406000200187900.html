note
  • The coating film structure indicates top coating only (S: solid, 2M: 2 coat metallic, 2P: 2 coat pearl, 3P: 3 coat pearl).
  • For painting, inner panel colours should be similar to the outer panel colours.
  • 2-tone colour is a slash (/) A front shows a body upper colour and the back shows a lower colour.
  • The fog lamp bezel painting section is coated in silver (AC11169).
  • The roof rails are coated in silver (AC11169).
