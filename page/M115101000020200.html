VARIABLE INDUCTION CONTROL SOLENOID VALVE CHECK <6G7>


1.Disconnect the vacuum hoses from the variable induction control solenoid valve.
note When disconnecting the vacuum hose, always make sure that it can be reconnected at its original position.
2.Disconnect the harness connector.

3.Connect a hand vacuum pump to nipple (A) of the variable induction control solenoid valve.
4.As described in the chart below, check airtightness by applying a vacuum with voltage applied directly from the battery to the variable induction control solenoid valve, and without applying voltage.

5.Measure the resistance between the terminals of the variable induction control solenoid valve.
Standard value: 29 - 35 Ω (at 20°C)
6.Replace the solenoid if airtightness or resistance is out of specification.
