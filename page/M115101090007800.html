SWIRL CONTROL VALVE SOLENOID CHECK <4M4 without fuel filter switch>


1.Disconnect the vacuum hoses from the swirl control valve (SCV) solenoid.
note When disconnecting the vacuum hose, always make sure that it can be reconnected at its original position.
2.Disconnect the harness connector.

3.Connect a hand vacuum pump to nipple (B) of the swirl control valve (SCV) solenoid.
4.As described in the chart below, check airtightness by applying a vacuum with voltage applied directly from the battery to the swirl control valve (SCV) solenoid, and without applying voltage.

5.Measure the resistance between the terminals of the swirl control valve (SCV) solenoid.
Standard value: 29 - 35 Ω (at 20°C)
6.Replace the solenoid if airtightness or resistance is out of specification.
