CENTRE DISPLAY



  • On the centre display, the clock, calendar, driving information (vehicle speed, fuel consumption, etc.), environmental information (altitude, air pressure, etc.), and audio information can be displayed. In addition, the user settings can be made.
  • Five languages (English, Spanish, German, French, Italian) are available to select. (Default: English)
  • The freeze alarm function has been equipped.
  • For the screen, the high-intensity VFD has been adopted to secure the visibility with high contrast. At the same time, the screen has been installed on the upper centre of the instrument panel to reduce the view point movement and the load applied to the driver, improving the safety.


  • The compass sensor has been installed to the upper area of the back door.
