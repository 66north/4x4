FUNCTION



 When the item comes up the warning start point of warning criteria based on the predetermined schedule, the service reminder indicator on the odometer and tripmeter display illuminates for 15 seconds. However, even if the illumination condition is met, the indicator does not illuminate immediately. It illuminates next time when the ignition switch is turned ON. To find the inspection timing, press the reset switch for switching the odometer and tripmeter display. When the service reminder indicator achieves the warning end point of warning criteria, it will be turned off by the M.U.T.-III special function or the reset switch special operation*.
note The service reminder function is disabled at shipment. When any of the following conditions is met, the function becomes available. When delivering the vehicle to the customer, enable the service reminder function, and check that the schedule is "GCC/EXP".

  • With the reset switch being pressed, the ignition switch is tuned to the "ON" position.
  • The reading on the odometer reaches to 100 km or 60 miles.
note Using the M.U.T.-III special functions or the reset switch special operation*, the schedule can be changed and the service reminder function can be made unavailable.
note *: For details about the rest switch special operation, refer to the Workshop Manual.

 Elapsed time (month)
 For the first time, alarm is not given by elapsed month. After the first alarm (1,000 km or 600 miles of driving), alarm is given every 6 months. Until the first alarm is finished, the number of remaining months is not displayed on the odometer/tripmeter.
Driving distance (km)
 The alarm is given for the first time when 1,000 km of driving is reached, and after that, it is given every 10,000 km of driving.
Driving distance (miles)
 The alarm is given for the first time when 600 miles of driving is reached, and after that, it is given every 6,000 miles of driving.
Optional INT
 The optional schedule can be set. (Only M.U.T.-III can be set.)

 Without function. "OFF" is displayed on the odometer/tripmeter.
