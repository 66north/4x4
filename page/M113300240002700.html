REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

<<A>> SET NO.1 CYLINDER TO TDC OF ITS COMPRESSION STROKE.
caution Never turn the crankshaft anti-clockwise.

Turn the crankshaft clockwise, align each timing mark to set No.1 cylinder to TDC (top dead centre) of its compression stroke.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT

>>A<< FUEL INJECTION PUMP, FLANGE PLATE AND FUEL INJECTION PUMP GEAR ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

Turn the crankshaft clockwise, align each timing mark to set No.1 cylinder to TDC (top dead centre) of its compression stroke.

1.Install the front plate after mating marks on the fuel injection pump gear and the flange plate so that the fuel injection pump gear cannot be rotated. It’s installed in front plate so that fuel injection pump gear doesn’t revolve together with a match mark of fuel injection pump gear and flange plate.
