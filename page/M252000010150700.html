GENERAL INFORMATION


The interior features luxurious look, designed considering passengers’ utility, roominess, and safety. Various measures have been taken actively to protect the environment and recycle resources.

FEATURES


Quality improvement
  1. Fully trimmed interior
  2. Use of soft leather to the armrest
  3. Adoption of aluminium design for the ornament panel, shift lever area, cup holder and so on
Usability improvement
  1. Sunvisors with ticket holder and vanity mirror
  2. Assist grips with storage compartments
  3. Passenger access grip
  4. Large rear floor console box (with front and rear sliding armrest)
  5. Heated seats <Option>
  6. Multi-arrangement seats
  7. Second separate seats (6/4 arrangement)
  8. Third seats (with removal and bottom-retracting mechanisms) <Long wheelbase>
  9. Movable ashtray (between front floor console and driver’s cup holder, etc.)
  10. Armrest (rear floor console, second seat)
  11. Cup holder (rear floor console, rear seat armrest)
  12. Tonneau cover <Vehicles with tonneau cover>
  13. Seat belt hook
  14. Luggage hook
Convenient boxes
  1. Glove box with key
  2. Card holder and box with a lid (centre panel)
  3. Seatback pocket (front seat)
  4. Armrest box
  5. Rear floor console box
  6. Rear side box (lower quarter trim LH) <Long wheelbase>
  7. Door pocket (front door trim)
  8. Luggage floor box
Safety improvement
  1. ISO-FIX child seat fixing bar (second seat outside)
  2. Tether anchorage (second seat)
  3. ELR 3-point seat belt (front, rear)
  4. Seat belt retractor double pretensioner and variable force limiter (front)
  5. Headlining energy absorption trim <Vehicles without curtain air bag>
  6. Automatic anti-glare mirror <Option>
Consideration for the optimum driving position
  1. Motor-driven power seats <Option>
  2. Height adjustment function exclusive to seat cushion (Manual seat)
  3. Seat height adjustment (power seat)
  4. Headrestraint with height adjustment (front, rear)
  5. Adjustable seat belt anchors for the front seat belts
Measures for resource recycling
  1. Aggressively use PP materials that are easy to recycle and easy to stamp material symbols on the plastic (resin) parts.
  2. Reduction of chemical material (formaldehyde*, organic solvent)
note *: Clear and colourless, toxic, irritating odour
