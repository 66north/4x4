EQUIPMENTS


IMMOBILIZER SYSTEM


<3,200>

<3,800>

The engine immobilizer system prevents the engine from starting and immobilizes the vehicle if a key other than the key registered for that vehicle is used in an attempt to start the engine after forced entry. The engine immobilizer system consists of the ignition key, key ring antenna, immobilizer-ECU <3,200> or immobilizer-ECU with an antenna <3,800> and the engine-ECU <3,200> or engine-A/T-ECU <3,800>.

AUDIO AND NAVIGATION SYSTEM



 An unique panel audio system (FM/LW/MW radio and 6-disk CD changer, or FM/LW/MW radio and CD player) imparts a uniformity with the instrument panel. This system adjusts sound volume and quality automatically according to the vehicle driving conditions.

ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM has been adopted to the long wheelbase. ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM is the system which combines 11-channel high power audio amplifier (total output: 860 W) incorporating digital signal processor (DSP) and 12 speakers. The audio amplifier is installed in the rear quarter inner panel (LH).

Mitsubishi power sound system is adopted for the short wheelbase. Mitsubishi power sound system is the system which combines 6-channel high power audio amplifier (total output: 330 W) incorporating digital signal processor (DSP) and 8 (6 positions) speakers. The audio amplifier is installed in the rear quarter inner panel (LH). (Mitsubishi power sound system)

AUDIO REMOTE CONTROL SWITCH




A centralised switch is located on the steering wheel spoke to allow you to control the audio system remotely.

ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM <LONG WHEELBASE>





  • 12 speakers <Long wheelbase: ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>

    • Front and rear tweeters: 3.5 cm soft dome tweeter
    • Front door and rear door: 16 cm Mid-Bass speaker
    • Front speaker: 8 cm Mid-range speaker
    • Back door speaker: 8 cm Mid-range speaker
    • Subwoofer: 25-cm dual voice coil subwoofer

Mitsubishi power sound system <short wheelbase>





  • 8 speakers (6 positions) <Short wheelbase: Mitsubishi power sound system>

    • Front tweeter: 3.5 cm hard dome tweeter
    • Front door speaker: 16 cm pp (polypropylene) cone speaker
    • Rear speaker : 16 cm PP (polypropylene) coaxial 2 way speaker

ACCESSORY SOCKET


The plug-in type accessory socket has been installed for the convenient use of accessories.



  • Accessory sockets have been added to front panel assembly and rear floor console, lower quarter trim (RH).<Long wheelbase>
  • Accessory sockets have been added to lower center panel and rear floor console, lower quarter trim (LH).<Short wheelbase>
  • The maximum load is 120 W when a single accessory socket is used. When three accessory sockets are used simultaneously, the combined maximum load for three sockets is 120 W.
  • The accessory socket of the front panel assembly can be changed to the cigar lighter as an option.
