PASSIVE SAFETY


POWER WINDOW AND SUNROOF WITH SAFETY MECHANISM


If a trapped hand, hand, or other item is detected while the sunroof or any window is closing, the motor direction is automatically reversed for safety.

IMPACT SAFETY BODY RISE(REINFORCED IMPACT SAFETY EVOLUTION)


The front and rear structures to absorb high energy, and the highly tough cabin structure is adopted to reduce the risk of passenger injuries at front-, rear-, and side-impact collisions, secure the space for life protection, and facilitate rescuing passengers. The structures also have the following features:




The following construction has been adopted for the impact safety body RISE. This structure alleviates the shocks which may be experienced by passengers in the event of a front, rear or side impact and maintains enough space inside the passenger compartment to preserve life and to improve passenger rescue.

  1. The high energy-absorbing structure includes front sidemembers with a straight frame and a large cross-section.
  2. The high energy-absorbing structure includes crushable zones at the front and rear of the front sidemembers to disperse the force of an impact.
  3. The joints between the front pillars and front deck crossmember maintain the high rigidity of the cabin.
  4. Large reinforcements have been added to each pillar to increase the rigidity of the joints along the whole of the side of the vehicle body.
  5. The high energy-absorbing structure includes rear floor sidemembers with a straight frame and a large cross-section.

SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEM (SRS)


The driver’s and passenger’s (front) air bags, side-air bags, curtain air bags and seat belts with pre-tensioner have been standard equipped on all vehicles.
Dual stage air bag system has been adopted to both driver and front passenger sides. This is consisted of the front impact sensor, SRS-ECU and dual stage inflator. Air bag is deployed in two stages according to the strength of impact received.
An inflator that does not contain sodium azide has been adopted for all types of the air bag modules.


SRS FRONT AIR BAG


Front air bag is designed to supplement the front seat belts. It eliminates or reduces injury to the front passenger(s) by deploying air bag(s) in case of a frontal collision.

SRS SIDE-AIRBAG


Side-airbag systems in the front seats are activated when sideward impacts applied to the vehicle exceed a threshold to protect the occupants’ upper bodies.

SRS CURTAIN AIR BAG


The curtain air bag systems are activated when sideward impacts applied to the vehicle exceed a threshold, to protect the heads of the occupants in the front and second seats.

SEAT BELT WITH PRE-TENSIONER


The seat belts with pre-tensioner work simultaneously with the SRS. The seat belt incorporating the pre-tensioner automatically winds the seatbelt upon front impact to reduce forward shifting of the driver’s and passenger’s.
