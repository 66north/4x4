GENERAL INFORMATION


The following three types of speakers are available.

6 speakers





  • Front tweeter: 3.5 cm balanced dome tweeter
  • Front door speaker: 16 cm paper cone speaker
  • Rear speaker <short wheelbase> or rear door speaker <long wheelbase>: 16 cm paper cone speaker

8 speakers (6 positions) <Short wheelbase: Mitsubishi power sound system>





  • Front tweeter: 3.5 cm hard dome tweeter
  • Front door speaker: 16 cm Metallised pp (polypropylene) cone speaker
  • Rear speaker: 16 cm Metallised PP (polypropylene) coaxial 2 way speaker

12 speakers <Long wheelbase: ROCKFORD™ ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>





  • Front and rear tweeters: 3.5 cm soft dome tweeter
  • Front door and rear door: 16 cm Mid-Bass speaker
  • Centre speaker: 8 cm Mid-range speaker
  • Back door speaker: 8 cm Mid-range speaker
  • Subwoofer: 25-cm dual voice coil subwoofer

TWEETER





  • For vehicles with 6 speakers and 8 speakers (6 position), tweeters are installed in the delta covers.
    Compared to the balanced dome tweeter employed for the vehicles with 6 speakers, the hard dome tweeter for the vehicles with 8 speakers can play clearer and speedier treble.



  • For vehicles with 12 speakers, tweeters are installed in the delta covers and the rear door trim. <ROCKFORD™ ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>
    Compared to the balanced dome tweeter employed for the vehicles with 6 speakers, the soft dome tweeter for the vehicles with 12 speakers can play clearer and more ear-pleasing treble.

SPEAKER





16 cm speakers are installed on the front doors and rear doors <Long wheelbase> or lower quarter trim <Short wheelbase>.

  • Compared to the paper cone speaker employed for the vehicles with 6 speakers, the metallised PP (polypropylene) cone speaker is adopted for the front door of vehicles with 8 speakers, enabling the playback of crisper bass sound. In addition, the metallised PP (polypropylene) coaxial 2 way speaker is adopted for the rear speaker, and the speakers for treble and low bass are arranged along the same axle, achieving a well-balanced wide range sound.

  • For the vehicles with 12 speakers, the Mid-Bass speaker is employed for the front door and rear door speakers. For the Mid-Bass speaker, PP (polypropylene) paper cone is adopted. Furthermore, the large magnet is adopted, enabling the playback of crisper bass sound and also the life-like mid range sound.



For vehicles with 12 speakers, Mid-range speaker is installed on the back door and instrument panel. The mid-range speaker can be strengthened by adoption of the neodymium for magnet, enabling the playback of life-like vocal sound as well as of clear mid range sound. <ROCKFORD™ ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>

SUBWOOFER <VEHICLES WITH 12 SPEAKERS(ROCKFORD™ ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM)>

A 25-cm dual voice coil subwoofer is installed on the lower quarter trim (L.H.) in the luggage compartment, thus enhancing low-tone sound.
