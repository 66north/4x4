DISASSEMBLY

1. TORQUE CONVERTER ASSY REMOVAL
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the oil seal.
  • Be careful not to drop the torque converter assy.

Remove the torque converter assy from the transmission assy.

2. BREATHER TUBE REMOVAL

1.Remove the bolt in order to remove the breather tube, breather hose, and breather plug No. 1 from the transmission case.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 10 mm
2.Remove the O-ring from the breather tube.
3.Remove the O-ring from breather plug No. 1.

3. TUBE REMOVAL
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the case or adaptor.
  • Be careful not to bend the tube.
  • Never reuse the removed tube.

Remove the tube from the transmission case.

4. SPEED SENSOR (INPUT) REMOVAL
caution Be careful not to damage the speed sensor.

Remove the bolt to remove the speed sensor from the transmission case.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14 mm

5. SPEED SENSOR (OUTPUT) REMOVAL
caution Be careful not to damage the speed sensor.

Remove the bolt to remove the speed sensor from the transmission case.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14 mm

6. TRANSMISSION CONTROL SHAFT LEVER LH REMOVAL

Remove the nut and spring washer in order to remove the transmission control shaft lever LH from the manual valve lever shaft.

7. ELBOW REMOVAL

1.Remove the 2 elbows.
2.Remove the 2 O-rings from the elbows.

8. INHIBITOR SWITCH REMOVAL

1.Using a screwdriver, pry off the washer.
2.Remove the nut to remove the washer from the inhibitor switch.

3.
caution Be careful not to damage the inhibitor switch.
Remove the bolt (seal bolt) to remove the inhibitor switch from the transmission case.
SIZE: Bolt
M8 x 1.25 x 30 mm (Seal bolt)

9. CONVERTER HOUSING REMOVAL

1.Remove the 10 bolts.
SIZE: Bolt
A: M10 x 1.5 x 35 mm
B: M10 x 1.5 x 35 mm (Seal bolt)
C: M12 x 1.75 x 38 mm (Seal bolt)

2.
caution Be careful not to damage the fitting surface of the converter housing and the transmission case.
Using a plastic hammer, tap the converter housing to remove it.

10. TRANSMISSION CASE RR ADAPTOR REMOVAL

1.Remove the 8 bolts.
SIZE: Bolt
A: M10 x 1.5 x 40 mm (Seal bolt)
B: M10 x 1.5 x 50 mm (Seal bolt)

2.
caution Be careful not to damage the fitting surface of the transmission case RR adaptor and the transmission case.
Using a plastic hammer, tap the transmission case RR adaptor to remove it.

11. OIL SEAL REMOVAL
caution Be careful not to damage the transmission case RR adaptor.

Remove the oil seal from the transmission case RR adaptor.

12. OIL PAN SUB-ASSY REMOVAL

1.Remove the 20 bolts.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14.5 mm

2.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the fitting surface of the transmission case and the oil pan sub-assy.
  • Be careful not to deform the oil pan sub-assy.
Using a plastic hammer, tap the oil pan sub-assy to remove it.
3.Remove the oil pan gasket.

13. OIL CLEANER MAGNET REMOVAL

Remove the 4 oil cleaner magnets from the oil pan sub-assy.
note Examine the chips and particles in the oil pan sub-assy and on the oil cleaner magnet to determine what type of wear has occurred in the transmission.
Steel (magnetic) = bearing, gear and plate wear.
Brass (non-magnetic) = bushing wear.

23. PARKING LOCK PAWL BRACKET REMOVAL

Remove the 3 bolts to remove the parking lock pawl bracket.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14 mm

24. PARKING LOCK ROD SUB-ASSY REMOVAL

Disconnect the parking lock rod sub-assy from the manual valve lever sub-assy.

25. PARKING LOCK PAWL REMOVAL

1.
caution Be careful not to apply too much force to the torsion spring.
Pull out the parking lock pawl shaft and the torsion spring.
2.Using a screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the parking lock pawl shaft.
3.Remove the parking lock pawl.

26. MANUAL VALVE LEVER SHAFT REMOVAL

1.
caution Be careful not to damage the manual valve lever sub-assy.
Using a hammer and a screwdriver, cut off the spacer to remove it.

2.Using a hammer and a pin punch, drive out the spring pin.

3.Pull the manual valve lever shaft out through the transmission case, and remove the manual valve lever sub-assy.

27. OIL SEAL REMOVAL
caution Be careful not to damage the transmission case.

Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 oil seals.

28. OIL PUMP ASSY REMOVAL

1.Remove the 10 bolts from the transmission case.
SIZE: Bolt
M8 x 1.25 x 40 mm

2.
caution Be careful not to drop the oil pump assy from the transmission case.
Using the special tool Oil pump puller (MD998212), remove the oil pump assy.

3.Remove the O-ring from the oil pump assy.

SIZE: O-ring
Inner diameter (mm)
 Thickness (mm)

4.Remove the thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust bearing race No. 1 from the oil pump assy.

29. FORWARD CLUTCH ASSY & DIRECT CLUTCH ASSY REMOVAL

1.
caution
  • Be careful not to drop the forward clutch assy & direct clutch assy.
  • Be careful not to drop the one-way clutch No. 3.
Remove the forward clutch assy & direct clutch assy with the one-way clutch No. 3.

2.Remove the thrust bearing race and the thrust needle roller bearing from the forward clutch assy & direct clutch assy.

3.Remove the clutch hub thrust washer, one-way clutch No. 2 and the clutch drum thrust washer No. 2 from the forward clutch assy & direct clutch assy.

30. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 3 (B3) REMOVAL

1.Using a screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the transmission case.

2.Remove the flange, 4 discs, 4 plates and cushion plate.
note Check the number of disc. (Refer to TRANSMISSION INSPECTION-INSPECT BRAKE DISK SET No. 3 (B3) )

31. BRAKE PISTON RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSY NO. 3 & SECOND BRAKE PISTON & SECOND BRAKE CYLINDER REMOVAL

1.Remove the hole snap ring from the transmission case.

2.Remove the brake piston return spring sub-assy No. 3 & second brake piston & second brake cylinder with the one-way clutch No. 1, planetary carrier thrust washer No. 1 and the one-way clutch inner sub-assy.

3.Remove the one-way clutch inner race sub-assy and the planetary carrier thrust washer No. 1
4.Remove the one-way clutch No. 1 from the brake piston return spring sub-assy No. 3 & second brake piston & second brake cylinder.

32. FR PLANETARY GEAR ASSY REMOVAL

Remove the FR planetary gear assy and the planetary carrier thrust washer No. 2.

33. FR PLANETARY RING GEAR & FR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE & CENTRE PLANETARY RING GEAR REMOVAL

1.Remove the FR planetary ring gear & FR planetary gear flange & centre planetary ring gear.
2.Remove the thrust bearing race No. 3 and the thrust needle roller bearing from the FR planetary ring gear & FR planetary ring gear flange & centre planetary ring gear.

34. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 1 (B1) REMOVAL

Remove the flange, 4 discs and 4 plates.
note Check the number of disc. (Refer to TRANSMISSION INSPECTION-INSPECT BRAKE DISK SET No. 1 (B1) )

35. CENTRE PLANETARY GEAR ASSY AND PLANETARY SUN GEAR REMOVAL

1.Remove the centre planetary gear assy and planetary sun gear.
2.Remove the thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust bearing race No. 4 from the centre planetary gear assy.

36. BRAKE PISTON NO. 1 & BRAKE CYLINDER NO. 1 REMOVAL

1.Align the special tool Adapter (MB992320) with the centre to only push the brake piston return spring sub-assy.

2.Using the special tools and a screwdriver, remove the hole snap ring from the transmission case.

  • Base (MB992317)
  • Cylinder (MB992318)
  • Center bolt (MB992319)
  • Flange bolt (MB992322)

3.Remove the brake piston return spring sub-assy and the brake piston No. 1 & brake cylinder No. 1.

37. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 2 (B2) REMOVAL

1.Set the special tool Adapter (MB992321) on the brake piston return spring sub-assy.

2.Using the special tools and a screwdriver, remove the hole snap ring from the transmission case.

  • Base (MB992317)
  • Cylinder (MB992318)
  • Center bolt (MB992319)
  • Flange bolt (MB992322)

3.Remove the flange.

4.Remove the 4 discs, 3 plates and flange.
note Check the number of disc. (Refer to TRANSMISSION INSPECTION-INSPECT BRAKE DISK SET No. 2 (B2) )

5.Remove the brake piston return spring sub-assy No. 2.

38. BRAKE PISTON NO. 2 & BRAKE CYLINDER NO. 2 REMOVAL

Remove the brake piston No. 2 and brake cylinder No. 2 from the transmission case.

39. PLATE STOPPER SPRING REMOVAL

Remove the plate stopper spring from the transmission case.

40. INTERMEDIATE SHAFT REMOVAL

1.Remove the hole snap ring from the transmission case.

2.Remove the intermediate shaft with the one-way clutch inner race, one-way clutch No. 3, RR planetary ring gear flange sub-assy & RR planetary ring gear, thrust bearing race No. 7, thrust needle roller bearing and thrust bearing race No. 8 from the transmission case.
3.

Remove the one-way clutch inner race, one-way clutch No. 3, RR planetary ring gear flange sub-assy & RR planetary ring gear, thrust bearing race No. 7, thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust bearing race No. 8 from the intermediate shaft.

41. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 4 (B4) REMOVAL

Remove the 2 flanges, 8 discs and 7 plates.
note Check the number of disc. (Refer to TRANSMISSION INSPECTION-INSPECT BRAKE DISK SET No. 4 (B4) )

42. RR PLANETARY GEAR ASSY REMOVAL

Remove the RR planetary gear assy with the thrust bearing race No. 9, thrust needle roller with race bearing and thrust needle roller bearing.
