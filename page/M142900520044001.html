INSPECTION PROCEDURE B-6: Power Window Anti-trap Function does not work Normally. <LH drive vehicles>
























COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


Malfunction of the power window motor revolution detection sensor is suspected.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the power window motor
  • Damaged wiring harness and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the power window operating current.


Check that the power window operating current is normal (Refer to ).

Q. Is the check result normal?

Adjust the door window glass (Refer to ), and then go to Step 2.

Replace the defective power window motor which operating current are abnormal.

STEP 2. Confirm the power window learning function.


Check that the power window switch has learned the fully closed position of the windows.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.

Make the power window switch learn the fully closed position of the windows (Refer to ).

STEP 3. Determine a trouble spot.


Q. Which door does the power window anti-trap function fail on?

Driver’s door : Go to Step 4.

Front passenger’s door : Go to Step 7.

Rear right door <Long wheelbase> : Go to Step 13.

Rear left door <Long wheelbase> : Go to Step 19.

STEP 4. Connector check: F-02 power window main switch connector and F-06 front power window motor (LH) connector


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.

Repair the defective connector.

STEP 5. Check the wiring harness from F-02 power window main switch connector terminal Nos. 8, 9, 10, 12 to F-06 front power window motor (LH) connector terminal Nos. 6, 2, 4, 5.



  • Check the input and output lines to the revolution detection sensor for open or short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.

Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 6. Retest the system.


(1)Replace the power window main switch.
(2)Check that the driver’s power window anti-trap function works.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.

Replace the front power window motor (LH).

STEP 7. Connector check: F-14 front power window motor (RH) connector


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.

Repair the defective connector.

STEP 8. Resistance measurement at the F-14 front power window motor (RH) connector.


(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Continuity between F-14 front power window motor (RH) connector terminal No.6 and body earth.
OK: Continuity exists (2 Ω or less)

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 10.

Go to Step 9.

STEP 9. Check the wiring harness F-14 front power window motor (RH) connector terminal No.6 to body earth.


note Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-114 and repair if necessary.

  • Check the input and output lines to the revolution detection sensor for open or short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 12.

Repair the wiring harness.
