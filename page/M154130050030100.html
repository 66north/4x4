Inspection Procedure 3: The Personal Lamp does not Illuminate Normally. <Using SWS Monitor>


caution Whenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


























COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If the personal lamps do not illuminate or extinguish normally, wiring harness connector(s) or the bulb may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES

  • Malfunction of the interior lamp switches
  • Malfunction of the room lamp bulbs
  • Malfunction of the personal lamp bulbs
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

Q. Is the check result normal?
YES (Normal conditions are displayed for all the items) : Go to Step 3.
NO (Normal condition is not displayed for item No.03) : Refer to ETACS - Input Signal Chart .
NO (Normal condition is not displayed for item No.05, 07, 41 or 42) : Refer to ETACS - Input Signal Chart .
NO (Normal condition is not displayed for item No.21 and 22) : Refer to ETACS - Input Signal Chart .
NO (Normal condition is not displayed for item No.31 or 32) : Refer to ETACS - Input Signal Chart .

STEP 3. Retest the system.
Check which lamp does not illuminate when the lamp switches are operated.

Q. Which lamp does not illuminate?
YES (All of the lamps illuminate normally) : The procedure is complete.
NO (No lamps illuminate normally) : Go to Step 4.
NO (One of the personal lamp (RH or LH) does not illuminate normally) : Go to Step 8.

STEP 4. Check the personal lamp switches.
Check which position (ON or DOOR) of the personal lamp switch does not activate the relevant lamp.

Q. Which switch position does not activate the lamp?
YES (All of the lamps illuminate normally) : The procedure is complete.
NO (The lamp does not illuminates at neither the ON nor DOOR position) : Go to Step 5.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the DOOR position) : Go to Step 7.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the ON position) : Replace the personal lamp.

STEP 5. Connector check: D-419 ETACS-ECU connector and E-104 personal lamp (RH) connector

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 6. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.6 to E-104 personal lamp (RH) connector terminal No.2.
note Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-130, E-128, joint connector D-34 and junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?
Replace the personal lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 7. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.5 to E-104 personal lamp (RH) connector terminal No.3.
note Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-130, E-128, joint connector D-34 and junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?
Replace the personal lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 8. Connector check: E-104 personal lamp (RH) connector or E-109 personal lamp (LH) connector

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 9.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 9. Check the bulbs of the personal lamp.
Check that the personal lamp bulbs are not burned out.

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 10
Replace the personal lamp bulb.

STEP 10. Check the personal lamp switches.
Check which switch position (ON or DOOR) does not activate the personal lamp.

Q. Which switch position does not activate the lamp?
YES (All of the lamps illuminate normally) : The procedure is complete.
NO (The lamp does not illuminates at neither the ON nor DOOR position) : Go to Step 11.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the ON position) : Go to Step 12.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the DOOR position) : Go to Step 14.

STEP 11. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.6 to E-104 personal lamp (RH) connector terminal No.2 or E-109 personal lamp (LH) connector terminal No.2.
note Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-130, E-128, joint connector D-34 and junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply line for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?
Replace the personal lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 12. Connector check: E-102 room lamp connector

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 13.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 13. Check the wiring harness from E-102 room lamp connector terminal No.9 to E-104 personal lamp (RH) connector terminal No.1 or E-109 personal lamp (LH) connector terminal No.1.
note Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector E-128 and repair if necessary.

  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?
Replace the personal lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 14. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.5 to E-104 personal lamp (RH) connector terminal No.3 or E-109 personal lamp (LH) connector terminal No.3.
note Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-130, E-128, joint connector D-34 and junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?
Replace the personal lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.
