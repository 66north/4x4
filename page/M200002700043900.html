ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION


RECYCLING


Recyclable thermoplastic resin and recycled materials have been actively used, and multilayer materials have been integrated into thermoplastic resin.
Classification
 Main contents
Components made of recycled materials
 Chip materials produced during manufacturing process have been reused for the following components: Bumper, radiator grille, instrument panel and door trims.
Waste materials produced by other industries have been reused for the following components: Sound absorbing materials for dash panel or roof and floor carpet.
Polypropylene resin containing old paper has been used for the air cleaner case.
Components made of materials which can be easily recycled
 Thermoplastic resin has been used for the food weatherstrip.
Thermoplastic resin has been used for the bumper, radiator grille, instrument panel and door trims.
Material indication
 The material indication has been implemented for 100 g or more of resin and rubber parts.

REDUCTION OF MATERIAL BURDEN ON ENVIRONMENT


Lead-free materials have been used for the fuel tank, radiator, heater core, and rubber parts.
Item
 Main contents
Elimination of environmental burden materials
 Lead-free materials have been used for the components such as the fuel tank, radiator, heater core, battery cable terminal, wheel balance weight, power steering high-pressure hose, glass ceramic print, electrostatic coating, crank bearing metal, connecting rod bearing metal, crankshaft, connecting rod, harnesses, and intake valve seat.
PVC (polyvinyl chloride)-free materials have been used for the waterproofing film.
Ozone layer protection
 Air conditioner filled with new refrigerant (HFC 134a) has been installed.
Prevention of air pollution
 Organic solvent drainage amount for body painting has been reduced.
