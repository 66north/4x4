INTERIOR LAMPS


CONSTRUCTION DIAGRAM







  • A front room lamp, installed to the front part of the roof, has been equipped with the lens-push switch type map and room lamp which can be operated easily from the driver’s and front passenger’s seat, offering an excellent operability for turning on and off the lamp.
  • The interior lamp switch has been integrated into the front room lamp to improve operability. This interior lamp switch enables turning ON/OFF of all the front room lamp, personal lamps, ceiling lamp and foot lamp.
    note When the lens-push switch of the front room lamp is in the ON position, the front room lamp (map and room lamp) illuminates continuously even if the interior lamp switch is turned OFF.
  • The personal lamp which is lens-push switch type and illuminates the rear seat has been installed to above the second seat.
  • The luggage compartment lamp which illuminates the luggage room is installed to the upper part of the luggage room.
  • The glove box lamp which illuminates when the lighting switch is ON has been installed to the glove box. <Vehicles with multi centre display>
  • The ceiling lamp which always illuminates the floor console area has been adopted to the headlining in order to emphasise the superior feel. The ceiling lamp can be turned off by operating the interior lamp switch of the front room lamp. <GLX and GLS>
  • Onto the centre of the driver, front passenger and rear seats, the foot lamp (front) <Standard equipment: Vehicles for Brazil and South Africa, Option: GLX (Except for South Africa)> and the foot lamp (rear) [Short wheelbase <Standard equipment: Vehicles for South Africa>, Long wheelbase <Standard equipment: Vehicles for South Africa, Option: GLX (Except for South Africa) and GLS (Except for South Africa)>] have been installed for convenience and superior feel. The foot lamp can be turned off by operating the interior lamp switch of the front room lamp.
  • A ignition key cylinder illumination lamp has been added to the ignition key cylinder.
  • Door lamps have been added to the front and rear doors.
  • The vanity mirror lamps have been installed on the sun visors of driver’s and front passenger’s seats. <Standard equipment:GLS, Option: GLX (Except for South Africa)>
