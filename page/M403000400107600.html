FENDER SHIELD






note Refer to the Front End Crossmember/ Headlamp Support section on for the welding point with front end crossmember and headlamp support.




NOTE ON REPAIR WORK

INSTALLATION

1.When installing the new front floor sidemember, the front sidemember outer cannot be welded to the body side at some points, and thus remove the front pillar lower gusset from the front floor sidemember in advance.

2.When assembling the front floor sidemember (Right side), apply in advance adhesives to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.

    Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
    Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

3.When installing the new front pillar lower gusset, the front pillar lower gusset bulkhead cannot be welded to the body side at some points, and thus remove the front pillar lower gusset bulkhead from the front pillar gusset lower in advance, and install the front pillar lower gusset bulkhead first.

4.When assembling the front pillar lower gusset (Right side), apply in advance adhesives to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.

    Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
    Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

5.When installing the new front fender shield, apply the body sealant in advance to the areas shown in the figure.
