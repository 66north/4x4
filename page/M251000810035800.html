OUTSIDE MIRROR




Folding door mirrors have been adopted. They have the following features:

  • Electric retractable remote controlled door mirrors have been adopted.
  • Door mirrors with heater element have been adopted<GLS>.
  • Hydrophilic door mirrors have been adopted<GLS>.
  • Side turn-signal lamp integrated door mirrors have been adopted<GLS>.
  • On the mirror housing bottom, the approach lamp which illuminates the foot area when getting on/off at night-time has been adopted<GLS>.
  • Large size mirror glass has been adopted for easy confirmation of safety around the vehicle.
  • Low air resistance door mirror shape has been introduced.
  • The deflector has been installed on the mirror housing bottom. With this installation, rectification effect is achieved, and the raindrops on the door window glass are decreased.
  • The mirror base has been changed to the die casting type in order to increase the rigidity of the door mirror installation section. This reduces the vibration of the mirror while driving at high speed.

DESCRIPTION OF CONSTRUCTION AND OPERATION


Electric retractable remote controlled door mirror




When the folding/unfolding switch on the electric remote control mirror switch is turned ON while the ignition switch is in ACC or ON position, ETACS turns either of the door mirror fold relay or unfold relay ON for 16 seconds to fold or unfold the door mirrors. Even after the ignition switch is turned OFF, the door mirrors remain operable for 30 seconds.
If the folding/unfolding switch on the remote control mirror switch is turned ON while one of the door mirror relays (fold/unfold) is in operation, the other operating relay is turned ON after 0.1 second.
note Whether the door mirrors are at the folding or unfolding position is determined by ETACS according to the memory of the door mirror relay position (fold/unfold) operated previously. For this reason, if the mirrors are manually folded, they may not move when the folding/unfolding switch is pressed next time.

Automatic unfolding function




When the vehicle speed reaches 30 km/h with the ignition switch ON and the mirrors folded, ETACS turns the door mirror unfold relay (ON) for 16 seconds to unfold the door mirrors. However, the door mirrors do not return to their unfolded positions automatically if the ignition switch is turned from OFF to ON, and then the folding/unfolding switch on the remote control mirror switch is operated.
