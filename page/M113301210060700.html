caution
  • The reinstallation histories of the removed fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly are up to five times. To count how many times the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly is reinstalled, record the number of the reinstallation histories on the service booklet by adding this latest number of the histories, which is usually "1", to the previous one. Use a new fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly when the total reinstallation history numbers reach five times, or when the supply pump assembly or common rail assembly is replaced. In this case, record "a new fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly, the number of the reinstallation histories is zero" on the service booklet.
  • If a fuel leak is present in the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pump pipe once, do not reuse it and replace with a new one.
  • If the fuel injector assembly, common rail assembly or supply pump assembly is replaced, the contact surface (seal surface) between the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pump pipe, and the other side component is changed, do not reuse the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pump pipe and replace with a new one.
  • When the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly is reinstalled, confirm there is no foreign material on the seal surface or in the pipe and then install it not to deviate from the axis, fitting the seal surface.
  • When the injector assembly is replaced, register the injector identification code and perform the fuel injection learning (Refer to GROUP 00 - Precautions Before Service, What The Common Rail Engine Learns ).

REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS

<<A>> FUEL SUPPLY PUMP PIPE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
caution Because the fuel pressure in the fuel line is high, wait for 3 minutes or longer after the engine is stopped. Then, cover the fuel supply pump pipe with a rag or similar materials to prevent the fuel spill, and then disconnect the fuel supply pump pipe assembly.

<<B>> FUEL INJECTION NO.4 PIPE ASSEMBLY/FUEL INJECTION NO.3 PIPE ASSEMBLY/FUEL INJECTION NO.2 PIPE ASSEMBLY/FUEL INJECTION NO.1 PIPE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL

Using special tool fuel injection pipe wrench (MB992188), remove the fuel injection pipe assembly No.1 to No.4.

<<C>> COMMON RAIL ASSEMBLY REMOVAL

caution
  • When operating, do not support the common rail assembly by holding the rail pressure limiter and the pressure sensor at both ends.
  • Before the installation, make sure that there is no foreign object or rust to the thread and to the high-pressure pipe sheet surface.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< INJECTOR ASSEMBLY/ SUPPORT BOLT/INJECTOR SUPPORT/INJECTOR HOLDER BOLT/GASKET/FUEL LEAKAGE PIPE ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1.Tighten the support bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 30 ± 1 N·m
2.Install the gasket, injector assembly and pivot boss.

3.Apply the engine oil to the bearing surface and thread of injector holder bolts.
4.Temporarily fix the injector supporters.
5.Install the gaskets to the fuel leakage pipe assembly.
6.Tighten the eye bolts at the Injector assembly side to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 15 ± 2 N·m
7.Tighten the eye bolt at the cylinder head side to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 15 ± 2 N·m
8.Tighten the fuel leakage pipe assembly mounting bolt to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 24 ± 4 N·m

9.Tighten the injector holder bolts according to the following procedure.
(1) Tighten the Injector holder bolts to 12 ± 1 N·m.
(2)
caution
  • If the bolt is tightened less than the specified lower limit of 90 degree angle, the bolt may become loose. Be sure to tighten correctly.
  • If the bolt is tightened in excess of the specified upper limit of 95 degree angle, loosen the bolt completely and repeat the entire procedures.
Using special tool angle gauge (MB991614), tighten the Injector holder bolts in the illustrated sequence by a further 90 to 95 degree angle.

>>B<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION

1.Using a proper socket wrench, the oil seal is pressed and fit into the rocker cover.

>>C<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION

Use special tools oil seal installer (MB992104) and handle (MB992075) to press-fit the oil seal.

>>E<<FUEL INJECTION PIPE ASSEMBLY/FUEL SUPPLY PUMP PIPE ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

caution

Loosen the common rail assembly mounting bolts so that the common rail assembly will be in the temporarily installed state.
2.Align the fuel injection pipe centre axis with the other side components (fuel injector assembly and common rail assembly) centre axis to install the fuel injection pipe. Then, tighten the flare nut temporarily until the tip of pipe (seal surface) contacts with the other side components.
note When the fuel injection pipe is installed, tighten the flare nut while shaking the pipe lightly to align the centre axis of both components.

Tightening torque: 20 ± 2 N·m
5.Tighten the common rail assembly mounting bolt to the standard torque.
6.Tighten the bolt of the inlet manifold installation point of the fuel return pipe to the standard torque.
7.Install the fuel return pipe bracket.
8.Tighten the bolt of the fuel return pipe bracket installation point of the fuel return pipe to the standard torque.
9.Install the inlet manifold air inlet fitting (Refer to GROUP 15 - Inlet Manifold ) <Except emission regulation Euro 5.
10.Install the inlet manifold air inlet fitting (Refer to GROUP 15 - Inlet Manifold ) <Emission Regulation Euro 5.
11.Align the fuel supply pump pipe centre axis with the other side components (common rail assembly and supply pump assembly) centre axis to install the fuel supply pump pipe. Then, tighten the flare nut temporarily until the tip of pipe (seal surface) contacts with the other side components.
note When the fuel supply pump pipe is installed, tighten the flare nut while shaking the pipe lightly to align the centre axis of both components.
12.Tighten the common rail assembly side flare nut in the fuel supply pump pipe and the supply pump assembly side flare nut alternately in several steps to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 35 ± 5 N·m
