EQUIPMENTS


IMMOBILIZER SYSTEM


<Diesel powered vehicle>

<Petrol powered vehicle>

The engine immobilizer system prevents the engine from starting and immobilizes the vehicle if a key other than the key registered for that vehicle is used in an attempt to start the engine after forced entry. The engine immobilizer system consists of the ignition key, key ring antenna, immobilizer-ECU <Diesel powered vehicle> or immobilizer-ECU with an antenna <Petrol powered vehicle> and the engine-ECU <M/T> or engine-A/T-ECU <A/T>.

AUDIO AND NAVIGATION SYSTEM


Three types of audio have been installed. For 2DIN-size, the 6-disk CD changer with radio and the CD player with radio which were designed to create a sense of uniformity with the instrument panel have been installed. For 1DIN-size, the tape player with radio has been installed. For 2DIN size audio equipment, a new function has been adopted that automatically corrects the sound quality and volume during driving to those of when the vehicle is stationary.



Separate amplifier (11-channel high power audio amplifier incorporating DSP with general output of 860 W) is located on the back of the subwoofer box assembly to adopt ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM. <Vehicles with 12 speakers>

AUDIO REMOTE CONTROL SWITCH




A centralised switch is located on the steering wheel spoke to allow you to control the audio system remotely.

ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM (COMPATIBLE WITH 5.1 CHANNEL SOUND)





  • 12 speakers <ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>

    • Front and rear tweeters: 3.5 cm soft dome tweeter
    • Front door and rear door: 16 cm Mid-Bass speaker
    • Instrument panel: Mid-range 8 cm speaker
    • Back door: 8 cm Mid-range speaker
    • Subwoofer: 25-cm dual voice coiled
