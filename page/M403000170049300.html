FRONT DOOR OUTER PANEL (ADHESION TYPE) <LONG WHEELBASE>







    Adhesive 1: Urethane body sealer
    Brand: 3M ATD Part No.08360, 08684, 08782 or equivalent

    Adhesive 2: Epoxyayresin adhesive
    Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


INSTALLATION
1.When installing the front door outer panel, grind and remove the paint from the bonded surface of the outer panel to ensure the bonding strength. After the removal, degrease it.
2.Install the outer panel and hem it. Wipe off the excessive adhesive and finish it.
