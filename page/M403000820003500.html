QUARTER OUTER <LONG WHEELBASE>

Part numbers referenced

MR484672





caution When repairing the area using sound dampening foam material do not use firing tools since the sound dampening foam material may burn.


NOTE ON REPAIR WORK

REMOVAL

1.When removing the quarter outer extension upper, to secure the strength of the rear pillar cut area, cut the quarter outer extension upper 50 mm below the side outer panel cut area.

2.When removing only the quarter outer extension lower (LH), the quarter outer extension upper (LH) interferes with it, and thus cut the area of the quarter outer extension lower (LH) in advance as shown in the figure, and bend it inward.
3.When replacing only the rear lamp housing (RH), the quarter outer extension lower (RH) interferes with it, and thus remove the quarter outer extension lower (RH) in advance.

INSTALLATION

1.When installing the new rear lamp housing (RH) part, apply the body sealant in advance to the area as shown in the figure.

2.When installing the new quarter outer extension lower parts, apply the adhesive in advance to the area as shown in the figure.

    Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
    Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

3.When installing the quarter outer panel, apply the body sealant in advance to the areas as shown in the figure. Also, to prevent the sound dampening foam materials to be filled in the next process from dripping, machine the body loose panel cushion (MR484672) to adhere it to the body side so that the wheel arch inside is filled.

    Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
    Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

4.Assemble the rear quarter outer panel, then cover the hole and flange with bolt and aluminium tape and fill the hole with sound dampening foam materials as shown in the figure of the instructions.

    Sound dampening foam material:
    3M ATD ULTRAPRO Panel foam-Yellow

5.Wait 2 hours after filling the sound dampening foam material to remove the bolt and aluminium tape, then melt the sound dampening foam material with a soldering gun so a clip, etc. can thoroughly be inserted in the hole filled with sound dampening foam material.

note Partial replacement is possible depending on the range of damage.
