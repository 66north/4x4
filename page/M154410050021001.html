SPEAKER TEST <RADIO AND CD PLAYER OR CD CHANGER>


RADIO AND CD PLAYER OR CD CHANGER SPEAKER CONNECTION CHECK MODE


Enter the speaker connection check mode according to the following steps:
1.Turn the Ignition switch to the "ACC" or "ON" position and switch off the radio and CD player or CD changer.

2.Press the following buttons in that order within sixty seconds from step (1).
(1) [CH1] button.
(2) Rewind button.
(3) Fast-forward button.
(4) [CH6] button.

3.Check that the speaker, which is displayed on the centre display unit, sounds (If the [CH6] button is pressed, the speaker will be changed).
Display
 Sound speaker
FL
 Front door speaker (LH) and front tweeter (LH)
FR
 Front door speaker (RH) and front tweeter (RH)
RL
 Rear door speaker (LH) <Long wheelbase> or rear speaker <short wheelbase> ,and rear tweeter (LH)
RR
 Rear door speaker (RH) <Long wheelbase> or rear speaker <short wheelbase> ,and rear tweeter (RH)
WOOFER
 Subwoofer
CENTRE
 Centre speaker
RG/L
 Back door speaker (LH)
RG/R
 Back door speaker (RH)
4.If a button other than the [CH6] button is pressed, or the ignition switch is turned to "LOCK" (OFF) position, you will exit from the speaker connection check mode.

BUZZ NOISE CHECK MODE
Inserting a CD during the speaker connection check mode shifts the mode to the buzz noise check mode, and the buzz noise sound of each speaker can be checked.
As with the speaker connection check mode, press the [CH6] button to switch the output speaker of the buzz noise check sound.
Check that the buzz noise sound is output from the output speaker of buzz noise check sound.
