BASE ENGINE


CYLINDER HEAD




A cylinder head made of an aluminum alloy, which is lightweight and offers a high level of cooling efficiency, has been adopted.

CYLINDER HEAD GASKET




The cylinder head gasket is a metal gasket which has been selected to have the appropriate thickness to match the projection amounts of the pistons. The thicknesses of the gaskets can be identified by the differences in the shape of notches A.

CRANK CASE




The crankcase made of cast iron is used. The crankcase is divided into the upper crankcase and the lower crankcase.
The upper crankcase and the lower crankcase are connected holding the crankshaft between the upper crankcase and the lower crankcase.

PISTON




The piston uses special aluminum alloy to improves heat efficiency and achieve lower weight. This improves engine performance and fuel efficiency.
The piston pinhole center is offset by 0.5 mm from the piston center towards the thrust side. Oversized types of 0.50 mm and 1.00 mm are also available.
The piston skirt outside circumference is given striation-like finishing to get better oil holding property and superior endurance against burns

CONNECTING ROD




Connecting rods are made of malleable high rigidity carbon steel. The rod portion has an H-shaped cross-section.
Oil supply to the connecting rod large end bearing is done through the oil hole from main journal provided on crankshaft towards crankshaft pin.

CRANKSHAFT BEARING




The three kinds of the oversize thrust bearing, 0.15mm, 0.30mm and 0.45mm, are supplied.

TIMING TRAIN




The timing gears have mating marks as "1", "3", "5", "6" and " · " on them so that the various gears can be engaged in the correct positions when they are installed.
The timing chain is an endless chain consisting of 98 links which is installed to the camshaft sprockets to the idler sprocket.

VALVE




The valve is made of heat resistant steel and has nitride processing on its entire surface.
