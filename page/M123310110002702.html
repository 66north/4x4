38. CLEAN CONTACT SURFACE
1.Remove any sealant and be careful not to get oil on the contact surfaces of the transmission case and the transmission case RR adaptor.
2.Clean the contact surfaces of the transmission case and the transmission case RR adaptor and the 6 bolt holes indicated in the illustration.
note Completely remove sealant and oil with white gasoline or equivalent.

39. TRANSMISSION CASE RR ADAPTOR INSTALLATION
caution
  • Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant. Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
  • Make sure the starting point and the ending point are about the middle between the bolts.

1.Apply FIPG to the transmission case RR adaptor as shown in the illustration.
Specified sealant: Three bond 1281B or equivalent
note Spread FIPG over the contact surface.

2.Install the transmission case RR adaptor to the transmission case.
3.Temporarily tighten the 8 bolts by hand as shown in the illustration.
SIZE: Bolt
A: M10 x 1.5 x 50 mm (Seal bolt)
B: M10 x 1.5 x 40 mm (Seal bolt)
note
  • Do not apply FIPG to the transmission case.
  • The letter A and B shown in the illustration indicate the kind of bolts.
4.Tighten the transmission case RR adaptor mounting bolts to the specified torque 34 ± 7 N·m.
