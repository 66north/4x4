REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS

<<A>> AXLE NUT REMOVAL
caution Do not apply pressure to wheel bearing by the vehicle weight to avoid possible damage to wheel bearing before tightening drive shaft nut fully.

Use special tool front hub & flange yoke holder (MB990767) to fix hub and remove the axle nut.

<<B>> REAR CALIPER ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1.Remove the rear caliper assembly with the brake hose.
2.Secure the removed rear caliper assembly with a wire or other similar material at a position where it will not interfere with the removal and installation of the knuckle assembly, rear hub assembly and backing plate.

<<C>> BRAKE DISC REMOVAL

If the brake disc is seized, install a M8 × 1.25 mm bolts as shown, and remove the disc by tightening the bolts evenly and gradually.

<<D>> LOWER ARM AND KNUCKLE DISCONNECTION

Use the floor jack to remove the lower arm mounting bolt by compressing the coil spring.

<<E>> NUCKLE/TOE-CONTROL ARM/UPPER ARM DISCONNECTION
caution
  • Do not remove the nut from ball joint. Loosen it and use special tool to avoid possible damage to ball joint threads.
  • Hang special tool with cord to prevent it from falling.

1.Install special tool ball joint remover (MB991897 or MB992011) as shown in the figure.

2.Turn the bolt and knob as necessary to make the jaws of special tool parallel, tighten the bolt by hand and confirm that the jaws are still parallel.
note When adjusting the jaws in parallel, make sure the knob is in the position shown in the figure.
3.Tighten the bolt with a wrench to disconnect the tie rod end.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< UPPER ARM AND KNUCKLE ASSEMBLY CONNECTION
If the upper arm ball joint is disengaged from the knuckle, always renew the upper arm ball joint dust cover and retainer (Refer to GROUP 34 - Upper Arm ).

>>B<< LOWER ARM AND KNUCKLE CONNECTION

Use the floor jack to install the lower arm mounting bolt by compressing the coil spring.

>>C<< AXLE NUT INSTALLATION
caution Do not apply pressure to wheel bearing by the vehicle weight to avoid possible damage when axle nut is loosened.

Using special tool front hub and flange yoke holder (MB990767), tighten the driveshaft nut.
Tightening torque: 220 ± 10 N·m

>>D<< AXLE CAP INSTALLATION

1.If axle cap is as shown after it is fitted, turn it at least one notch clockwise to align it with the split pin hole.

2.If the axle cap is as shown, turn it at least one notch anticlockwise to align it with the split pin hole.
3.If the split pin hole is not visible, turn it at least three notches clockwise to align it with the split pin hole.
