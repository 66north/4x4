FRONT HEATER UNIT AND FRONT BLOWER ASSEMBLY






The following front blower unit and the front heater unit have been adopted.

  • The heater and cooling units have been combined in a single unit.
  • The shape of the front blower unit has been optimised.
  • The clean air filter with deodorising function working for VOC (volatile organic compound) has been adopted to improve the air quality inside the passenger compartment.
  • Clean air filter is installed to the backside of the glove box to facilitate the filter replacement.
  • With the installation of PTC heater*, the heating performance is ensured. <3200 option>
note *Positive temperature coefficient (Electric heater with positive temperature characteristic)

OPERATION




Air flows inside the front heater unit along two levels: one that goes through the heater core, and one that bypasses the heater core, and temperature is controlled by a single air mixing damper. In addition, the two-level flow full air mix heater has a low air flow resistance, so that the air flow volume could be increased and operating noise could be reduced.
