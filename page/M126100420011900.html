DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINT

<<A>> BEARING REMOVAL

1.Bend the outside periphery of dust cover inward with a wooden hammer.

2.After special tool rear axle shaft bearing remover (MB990560) has been installed as shown, tighten the nut of the special tool MB990560 until the portion of special tool MB990560 touches the bearing outer race.

3.Press out the inner shaft from the bearing.

REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< DUST SEAL INSTALLATION

Press-fit the new dust seal into the housing tube by using the following special tools, until it is flush with the housing tube end face.

  • Installer bar (MB990938)
  • Oil seal installer (MB990955)

>>B<< DUST COVER INSTALLATION

Using a steel pipe, install a new dust cover onto the inner shaft.
