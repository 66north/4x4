GENERAL INFORMATION


The V5AWF automatic transmission is adopted for vehicles with 4M4 engine. It has the following features for improvements from the conventional A/T.

  • The slip lock-up control, which is a first for vehicles with 4M4 engine, and the reviewed lock-up region improves fuel economy.
  • The new automatic transmission fluid (DIA QUEEN ATF-PA) is adopted. This ATF lowers viscosity in the actual operating temperature range and reduces drag loss, improving the fuel economy. With the reduced drag loss, the low temperature starting performance of the engine is improved.
  • The A/T fluid filler pipe and level gauge is abolished.
