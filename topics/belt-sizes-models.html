Belt Part Numbers — Serpentine & Timing Belt Cross-Reference

● Moderate ⏱ 20 min Gen 4 (2007–2013) 4M41 6G75 2008–2013

Compatible serpentine (accessory) belt and timing belt part numbers for the 2008–2013 Pajero IV. Serpentine belt (both engines): OEM MD161162, profile 6PK1850 (6-rib poly-V, 1850mm). Timing belt (6G75 petrol): OEM MD360360, 1440mm / 108-tooth. Replace every 120,000 km. Timing belt (4M41 diesel): OEM MD362047, 1600mm / 80-tooth. Replace every 120,000 km. WARNING: Timing belt failure destroys the engine. Never skip or delay this service.

Tips & common mistakes

  • Timing belt replacement is professional-only work. Incorrect installation causes engine failure (bent valves, broken pistons).
  • Always replace the tensioner pulley and water pump when doing the timing belt. The extra parts cost $80–150 but save a second labour bill if either fails.
  • A cracked or glazed serpentine belt squeals at cold start. This is your warning — replace before it breaks and leaves you stranded.
  • Serpentine belt is DIY-friendly (20–30 min). Timing belt is not — always use a mechanic with Mitsubishi experience.
  • The 4M41 diesel and 6G75 petrol use different timing belt profiles and lengths. Always confirm engine type before ordering.
  • Keep your serpentine belt service receipt. If it breaks within the expected interval, the supplier may honour a warranty claim.
