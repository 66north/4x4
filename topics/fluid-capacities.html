Fluid Capacities — Quick Reference

● Easy ⏱ 5 min Gen 4 (2007–2013) 4M41 6G75 2008–2013

Complete fluid capacity reference for the 2008–2013 Pajero IV. Use these figures when performing drain-and-refill services. Always verify with dipstick or sight glass after filling — actual capacity varies slightly depending on how completely the old fluid drains.

Parts needed

PartOEM / SpecBuy online
Engine oil — 4M41 diesel (with filter change)
Fill to MIN mark, run 30 seconds, recheck. Overfilling causes oil burning.
 5W-30 ACEA C3 (DPF) or 5W-40 ACEA B4 (non-DPF) 6.5–7.0 L
Engine oil — 6G75 petrol (with filter change)
Fill to MIN mark first, run engine briefly, top to MAX. Do not overfill.
 5W-30 or 5W-40 API SL/SM 4.9 L
Automatic transmission fluid (Aisin 5-speed)
Check with engine warm, running, selector in Park. Use dipstick hot-check procedure.
 ATF SP-III (Mitsubishi Genuine or equivalent) 3.0 L drain-and-refill / 8.0–9.0 L full dry fill
Coolant — total system capacity
Fill expansion tank to MAX. Run engine with heater on full to purge air pockets, then top up.
 Mitsubishi Genuine or ASTM D3306 compliant 9.5 L (radiator + block + heater core + expansion tank)
Front differential oil
Fill until fluid drips from filler hole. Limited-slip diff requires GL-5 LS additive.
 API GL-5 80W-90 (or 75W-90 synthetic) 1.5 L approx.
Rear differential oil
Same levelling-plug fill method. Check for LSD (Torsen) — use GL-5 LS fluid if equipped.
 API GL-5 80W-90 (or 75W-90 synthetic) 2.0 L approx.
Transfer case fluid
Often overlooked. Change every 40,000 km. Same levelling-plug fill method.
 ATF SP-III or equivalent 1.0–1.2 L approx.
Power steering fluid
Check at MIN/MAX marks on reservoir. Top up only — full flush rarely needed.
 Mitsubishi Genuine PSF or Dexron II/III ATF 0.9 L reservoir (0.5 L typical top-up)
Brake fluid
Top up to MAX only. Do NOT mix DOT 3 and DOT 4. Flush every 60,000 km or 3 years.
 DOT 4 0.5 L reservoir (full system ~0.8 L)
Windscreen washer fluid
Use screen wash concentrate in cold climates to prevent freezing in reservoir and lines.
 Water + screen wash concentrate 3.5 L reservoir

Tips & common mistakes

  • Engine oil: always fill to MIN mark on dipstick, run engine 30 seconds, then recheck. Never trust volume alone.
  • Transmission: check level with engine warm and running, selector in Park. Hot-check procedure differs from cold.
  • Coolant: fill to MAX on expansion tank. Run engine with heater on full to purge air pockets, then top up.
  • Brake fluid: if topping up before pad replacement, do NOT fill to MAX — compressing caliper pistons will overflow the reservoir.
  • Differential oil: fill until fluid drips from the filler hole — that is the correct level, no guessing needed.
  • Transfer case fluid is the most commonly forgotten fluid on 4WD vehicles. Check it at every 40,000 km service.
