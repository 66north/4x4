Filter Cross-Reference — Oil, Air & Cabin Filter Part Numbers

● Easy ⏱ 15 min Gen 4 (2007–2013) 4M41 6G75 2008–2013

Compatible oil filter, engine air filter, and cabin air filter part numbers for the 2008–2013 Pajero IV. OEM part numbers: oil filter MD001163, engine air filter MD620035, cabin air filter 7803A019 (or 7803A027). All filters below are verified compatible with both 4M41 diesel and 6G75 petrol engines. Replace oil filter every 10,000 km, air filters every 20,000 km (more often in dusty conditions).

Tips & common mistakes

  • K&N 33-2164 reusable air filter breaks even at ~80,000 km versus disposable paper. Worth it on high-mileage vehicles.
  • Cabin air filter is a 10-minute no-tools job: pop out the glovebox and slide the filter out. No excuse to skip it.
  • In dusty or desert environments, replace the engine air filter every 5,000–10,000 km instead of 20,000 km.
  • A clogged engine air filter is one of the most common (and cheapest) triggers of the P0100 MAF sensor code.
  • Dirty cabin filter reduces AC cooling efficiency noticeably. If the fan is loud but airflow is weak, the cabin filter is likely blocked.
  • Do NOT clean a paper air filter with compressed air — this damages the fibres. Replace it.
