Battery Part Numbers — OEM & Aftermarket Cross-Reference

● Easy ⏱ 15 min Gen 4 (2007–2013) 4M41 6G75 2008–2013

Compatible replacement batteries for the Pajero IV (2008–2013), both petrol 6G75 and diesel 4M41. The correct spec is 95–100 Ah / 720–830 CCA (12V, top-terminal). OEM Mitsubishi part number is MN111155. All models below are direct drop-in replacements. Disconnect negative terminal first before replacing.

Tips & common mistakes

  • Always disconnect the negative (black) terminal first to prevent short circuits.
  • Terminal torque: 10 Nm on M8 bolts. Snug, not forceful — over-tightening cracks the battery case.
  • Verify alternator output after fitting: should read 13.2–14.4V at battery terminals with engine running.
  • Batteries from hot climates (>35°C) degrade faster. If the vehicle lived in a hot region, inspect or replace at 3 years.
  • 95–100 Ah is the correct range. Going below 80 Ah risks hard starts in winter; above 110 Ah can overload the alternator.
  • If the battery keeps dying, test the alternator before replacing the battery again.
