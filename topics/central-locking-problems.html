Central Locking & Remote Key Problems

● Moderate ⏱ 20 min Gen 4 (2007–2013) 4M41 6G75 2008–2013

Central locking and remote key faults on the Pajero IV fall into two categories: remote key (keyfob/transponder) issues and the physical locking actuator system. A dead key battery is the most common cause of remote failure. Actuator faults affect individual doors. The ETACS module (Electronic Time and Alarm Control System, Section 52 of the service manual) governs the entire body electronics including locking, alarm, and immobiliser.

When do you need this?

  • Remote key not working (no lock/unlock response)
  • One door not locking or unlocking with central locking
  • Alarm triggering unexpectedly or not arming
  • Immobiliser light flashing — engine won't start
  • Central locking works from inside switch but not from remote
  • Doors locking but not unlocking (or vice versa)

Tips & common mistakes

  • Replace the remote key battery first (CR2032 coin cell). This fixes 60% of remote key complaints. Key should respond within 1 metre of the vehicle after battery change.
  • After replacing the key battery, you may need to re-sync the remote: sit in the vehicle, insert key in ignition, press lock button 5 times within 10 seconds, then press unlock once. Confirm with a lock/unlock cycle.
  • If only one door fails, the fault is the door actuator motor for that door — not the ETACS module or remote.
  • The Pajero IV immobiliser uses a transponder chip in the key. A worn or damaged key may start the engine intermittently. Have the key copied before it fails completely.
  • Check fuse 12 (20A) in the engine bay fuse box for the ETACS/body control system if all locking functions fail simultaneously.
  • Water ingress in door actuators is common on older vehicles. A door actuator that works intermittently in dry weather but fails after rain usually has a corroded motor.
  • Alarm false triggers are often caused by a faulty bonnet/boot switch — the alarm thinks the bonnet is being opened. Spray the switch contacts with contact cleaner.
